Lobster Press — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

What is Lobster Press?

Lobster Press is a social media management tool used by Tuckerton Group LLC to publish content to its business Facebook and Instagram pages, including Tuckerton Lumber Company, Surfbox Portable Storage, and Keli Lynch KW Premier.

What data do we collect?

Lobster Press does not collect, store, or process any personal data from Facebook users. The app is used solely to publish posts to business pages managed by Tuckerton Group LLC.

How do we use Facebook data?

We use Facebook Page publishing permissions to post content (text and images) to our own business pages. We do not access, read, or store any data from Facebook users who interact with our pages.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data retention

We do not retain any Facebook user data. Post content is managed through our internal content management system.
